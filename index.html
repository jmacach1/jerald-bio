Jerald Macachor

Software Developer

U.S. Navy Veteran, Scientist, MBA

Bio

Jerald Macachor was born in November 11, 1987 in Cebu City, Philippines. He and his family moved to United States in 2004 in Las Vegas, Nevada. Shortly after graduating high school and attending college at UNLV for a year, Jerald enlisted in the U.S. Navy and served as a Hospital Corpsman.

Jerald served for 6 years in the Navy in San Diego California. He mainly assisted in surgery at the Naval Medical Center San Diego. He went to tour that brought him to 5 countries. He then used the GI Bill to pursue a Biology degree, and subsequently an MBA and Masters in Biotechnology. He learned programming from his Bioinformatics courses and used that skill to enter the Software industry.

Jerald currently resides in Irvine, California with his girlfriend wife Francesca "Franchy" and daughter Ada. He's taking a Full Stack Immersive Course at San Diego Global Knowledge University. He enjoys playing guitar, jiu jitsu, and surfing.
